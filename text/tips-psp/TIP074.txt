#EN#1
In a unitary space, a linear transformation which preserves the value of the Hermitian inner product of two arbitrary vectors before and after the transformation.%N
Suppose we denote the adjoint of an n-by-n matrix M as M*.%N
Then M is unitary if it is a complex-valued square matrix fulfilling the equation M*M = MM* = E, where E is the identity matrix of order n.
