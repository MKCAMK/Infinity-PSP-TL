#EN#1
Refers to a new unit of information content, different from the classical bit (information represented by a binary digit, 0, off, or 1, on) used in modern computers (von Neumann machines).%N
A physical state in which "0" and "1" overlap (a quantum superposition) is treated as an operational unit.%N

#EN#2
Also known as a qubit. Computers that use them are called %TS072quantum computers%TE, and are capable of carrying out very fast calculations (massively parallel computation).
