#EN#1
A stream of plasma which emanates from the upper atmosphere
of the sun. It is composed mainly of electromagnetic waves
and protons, and moves at speeds of 350-700 kilometers per second.%N
# Comets which leave trails tracing to the far side of the sun 
# are blown there by the solar wind.%N
Comets always have their tails tracing away from the sun, blown there by the solar wind.%N
It does not reach as far as the surface of the Earth
and thus has no effect on terrestrial magnetism;
# however, magnetic storms, the aurora borealis, or disruptions in the ionosphere
# are all caused by this solar wind.
however, it can cause magnetic storms, the aurora borealis,
or disruptions in the ionosphere.
