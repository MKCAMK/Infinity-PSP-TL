#EN#1
A large overhanging edge of snow on the leeward side of a mountain ridge.%N
Larger ones can be in excess of ten meters.%N
Since the inside of a cornice is composed entirely of snow, it will collapse if trodden on by a human.%N
The presence of snow cornices make it very difficult to tell where the actual ridge ends.
