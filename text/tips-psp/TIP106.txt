#EN#1
The region surrounding each person, or the area which a person considers their domain or territory.%N
Often, when someone enters the range of your personal space, you will begin to feel uneasy or uncomfortable.%N
While it is highly variable and difficult to measure accurately, the best estimates for personal physical space place it around 60 centimeters to either side, 70 centimeters in front, and 40 centimeters behind the body for the average westerner.
