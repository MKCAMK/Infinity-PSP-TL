#EN#1
A type of projective psychological test.%N
The subject of the test is first asked to interpret a series of cards with ambiguous, bilaterally symmetric inkblot designs. The answers are then analyzed so as to try and determine that person's personality characteristics.%N
Invented by Rorschach (Hermann R., 1884-1922), a Swiss psychologist.
