#PARAGRAPHS_TRANSLATED:
#EN#1
On a high mountain at sunrise or sunset, when one is standing in front of a bank of fog, the sunlight shining from behind you may project your shadow onto the fog, surrounded by a ring of rainbow light.%N
This is called a Brocken specter.%N
The resulting gigantic shadows, like those of a monster, have been called the phantoms of the Brocken.%N
The name of the phenomenon comes from the Brocken, a mountain in Bavaria, Germany, where it has often been observed.
