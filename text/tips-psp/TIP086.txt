#EN#1
Means "he who is wished for" in Hebrew.%N
The Biblical Saul from whom this name is derived was the first king of ancient Israel.%N
He was truly the king whom the people had wished for, and he lived up to their expectations.%N
Certainly, as a king and as a warrior, he might never have known defeat.%N

#EN#2
However, he failed in his God-given mission to become an instrument of the Lord.%N
Though anointed by God to become "an instrument of the Lord," as the king of God's people, he did not. The reason is that Saul disobeyed the will of God....%N
Saul was forsaken by God, and met a tragic end.
