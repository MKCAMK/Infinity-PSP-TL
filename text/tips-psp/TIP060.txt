#PARAGRAPHS_TRANSLATED:
#EN#1
A part of the cerebral cortex, the hippocampus controls the functions of desire, instinct, the autonomic nerves, and so on.%N
Due to its responsibility for deciding the level of urgency of received information and storing memory in other parts of the brain, it is referred to as the "memory factory."%N
It is said that without the hippocampus, a person won't be able to remember what had happened even five minutes ago.%N

#EN#2
Additionally, it is not simply the case that "one becomes forgetful with age."%N
Adults, because of having a much greater store of memories than children, are said to take much longer to remember a single essential piece of information.
