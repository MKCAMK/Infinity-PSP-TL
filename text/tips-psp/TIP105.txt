#EN#1
When one is dreaming, yet at the same time conscious of the fact that he or she is currently dreaming.%N
It can also refer to the technique of entering such a dream.%N
It is said that those who are having lucid dreams realize that they are in a dream and are able to direct the continuation of the dream according to their own will.
