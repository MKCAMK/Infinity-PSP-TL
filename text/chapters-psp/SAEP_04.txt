
ゆにのもとを離れたオレは、まっすぐ内海のところへと向かった。%K%P
I left Yuni's side and went straight to Utsumi.

内海の胸には、双子の赤ん坊が抱きかかえられていた。%K%P
Utsumi was holding her twin babies against her chest.

赤ん坊は二卵性……。片方が男の子、片方が女の子だった。%K%P
They were fraternal twins.... A boy and a girl.

オレとこころは一緒になって、赤ん坊の頬を指先でつついた。%K%P
I joined Kokoro, and together we gently poked their soft cheeks with our fingertips.

ぷにぷにとした柔らかな感触に、心の中まで溶ろけ落ちてしまいそうだった。%K%P
That softness suffused me with a sense of warmth that melted all the way to my heart.

ふたりの赤ん坊は元気よく足を蹴っていた。%K%P
The two babies kicked their feet energetically.

愛らしいダンスのようにも見えた。%K%P
It looked like they were inventing a cute new dance.

彼らはただ、遊びたかっただけなのだろう。%K%P
Most likely they just wanted to play.

温かな羊水を漂いながら、わずかな夢幻に目醒め、無邪気な戯れに束の間、興じていただけなのだと思う。%K%P
They might've opened their eyes to a dream while floating in the amniotic fluid, spent a few moments innocently playing, trying only to amuse themselves.

無邪気、無垢、天衣無縫……。%K%P
Innocently, purely, artlessly....

まっさらな純白の心に悪意はなく、悪意を持たぬがゆえに良心もまた持たないのだった。%K%P
With brand-new pure hearts, free of ill will, holding no malice and totally without conscience.

赤ん坊が笑った。%K%P
The babies laughed.

それにつられて、こころも笑顔……。%K%P
Seeing that, Kokoro smiled as well....

オレの胸に去来する想いは慈愛……そして無常観……。%K%P
In my heart were recurring feelings of both affection... and uncertainty....

新しい命に触れた瞬間、オレは生滅流転のことわりを知った。%K%P
The instant I touched these new lives, I learned to deny the endless circle of life and death.
