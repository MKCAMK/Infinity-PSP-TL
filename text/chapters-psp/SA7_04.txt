Right... I was just thinking about that...

After a while, the me before me proposed something.

Say, what do you think would happen if we killed the me of the future?

榎本「ん？　そうだな……」%K%P
Hm? Let's see...

榎本「どうもしないさ。こいつの世界のお前の肉体が死ぬ」%K%P
Not much at all. Your body in his world dies.

Really, that's all.

So that means there'd be no consequences to the present me or you, right?

No, none.

榎本「まあ、もし仮に、未来の俺やお前が同じ結果に辿り着いてしまったとしたら、最悪だがな」%K%P
Well, I suppose it'd be very bad if, for example, you and I arrived at the same result...

'I' see.

But don't you feel like there's no point in worrying about that? It's nonsense.

That's right.

And I have confidence in it. Confidence that there's no way our future will turn out as wretched as that one.

So do 'I'.

Well then, will you let me take care of him?

Are you serious?

Yeah, of course.

'I' want to experience it at least once.

悟「自分を殺す、ってやつをね」%K%P
...Killing myself, that is.

悟「…………」%K%P
......

そんな会話をオレは朦朧とした意識の中で聞いていた。%K%P
That consciousness reached me though a haze.

自分を殺す？%K%P
Killing myself?

オレがオレを殺すっていうのか？%K%P
I'm going to kill me?

ははっ、何を馬鹿なことを言っているんだ。%K%P
Haha, what a foolish thing I'm saying.

そんなこと、好んでするやつがいるもんか。%K%P
As if I'd do that on purpose.

オレに自傷癖はないんだぜ……。%K%P
I don't have any such impulses for self-injury....

榎本「…………」%K%P
......

榎本「いいだろう、勝手にしろ」%K%P
All right, have it your way.

そう言って、榎本はポケットからサングラスを取り出してかけた。%K%P
Saying that, Enomoto took out his sunglasses from his pocket.

榎本「俺は別に関知しない。こいつは……」%K%P
I don't really care. He...

榎本「俺とは関係のない『オレ』だからな」%K%P
He is an "I" that <i>I</i> have nothing to do with.

悟「それはありがたい。では、好きにさせてもらうよ」%K%P
'I''ll thank you for that. Well then, 'I' suppose 'I''ll just do as 'I' please.

そう言うと、目の前のオレはポケットから何かを取り出した。%K%P
On saying that, the me before me took something out of his pocket.

それは飛び出し式のナイフだった。%K%P
It was a type of retractable knife.

目の前のオレの手にはそれが握られていた。%K%P
The me before me seized it in his hand.

まさか……。%K%P
No....

まさか、まさか、まさか……。%K%P
No, no, no....

本気……なのか……？%K%P
Are you... serious...?

オレはただ茫然と、目の前のオレとナイフを眺めていた。%K%P
Dumbfounded, I stared at the me before me and the knife in his hand.

現実感がまったくなかった。%K%P
I'd lost all sense of reality.

オレがオレを殺す？%K%P
I'm going to kill me?

そんなこと……あっていいわけが……。%K%P
Such a thing... there's no way....

And at that moment――

Guah!

The knife was thrust into my chest.

刃の部分は根元までめり込んでいる。%K%P
The blade sank in to the base.

The me before me was the one holding the knife.

全身の体重をかけるようにして、ぐいぐいとさらにナイフを突き入れてくる。%K%P
The other me pulled the knife back out and thrust it in again and again, making sure to put all the weight of his body in it.

苦しい。息が……息ができない。%K%P
It hurt. I... I couldn't breathe.

My chest was hot.

Burning. Melting.

The knife, stuck in my chest, burned my body like the flames of hell.

My vision distorted.

立っていられない。%K%P
I couldn't stand.

力が……入らない。%K%P
I couldn't... move.

グニャグニャに歪んだ視界の先に、オレの狂喜に満ちた顔があった。%K%P
With my distorted vision, I could see my own face before me, full of twisted joy.

オレの返り血を浴びて、真っ赤に染まった、オレが。%K%P
The one bathing in the shower of my spurting blood, slowly turning red, was me.

Guah... ghh, kkk, kkkkkkk, kkkkkkh... guwaaaaahhhhhh...

I screamed in agony, a death cry.

Say...

Somewhere beyond my impaired field of hearing, Enomoto asked the me who was stabbing me.

How does it feel?

悟「うむ……」%K%P
Hmm...

Not bad.

The me in front twisted his wrist, digging the knife further into me. And then, he ripped it out.

Gyah, gggh, GYAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAA！！！

胸からはガブガブと鮮血があふれている。%K%P
Fresh blood overflowed from my chest.

オレの視界は真紅に染まっていった。%K%P
My vision was dyed crimson.

悟「本当に、悪くないね」%K%P
Not bad at all.

It's like drinking the most delicious black coffee the world has to offer.

Ohh?

悟「やはり、コーヒーはブラックに限る」%K%P
Just as 'I' thought, nothing beats black coffee.

Don't you agree?

――Yukidoh?

ユ　ウ　キ　ド　ウ　？%K%P
Y U K I D O H?

え？　いま、なんて言った？%K%P
Eh? What did he say just now?

Indeed.

There is no need for impurities.

I don't.

Because I'm different from you.
