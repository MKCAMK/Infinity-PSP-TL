
またしても、こころに置き去りにされたオレ……。%K%P
After being left behind by Kokoro again, I....

仕方なく、とぼとぼと来た道を戻った。%K%P
I reluctantly trudged back up the path I'd come.

しばらく歩くと、懐かしい後ろ姿が見えてきた。%K%P
After a few steps, I saw the back of someone whom I'd been missing for the longest time.

懐かしいとは言っても、この７日間に何度も目にしたことはあるのだけれど……。%K%P
Well, even though I say I'd missed her "for the longest time," I guess I did see her several times over the last week....

彼女はポツンとひとりきり、渚にたたずんでいた。%K%P
She stood there, all alone, on the beach.

――Mayuzumi Lin.

オレがかつて交際していた彼女……。%K%P
The woman I was once together with....

まだ自分自身の姿となって、彼女と話したことはなかった。%K%P
I've yet to talk to her since I returned to my own body.

この身体で会話するのは、とても久し振りのことだった。%K%P
It's been a long time since we've held a conversation where I was in my own body.

オレは心を弾ませながら、鈴のもとへと歩いていった。%K%P
My heart beat faster and faster as I walked over to her.

彼女の正面にまわり込む。%K%P
She began to turn around.

探りを入れるように、彼女の顔色をうかがった。%K%P
I looked into her eyes, as if trying to sense how she felt.

なんて話しかければいいのだろう？%K%P
What should I say?

最初の言葉がなかなか見つからなかった。%K%P
I couldn't find the words to address her with.

やっとのことで唇から洩れた言葉は……。%K%P
The words that finally slipped out of my mouth were....

悟「久し振りだな、鈴」%K%P
Long time no see, Lin.

オレの方は、借り物の身体でなら何度も会ったことがある。%K%P
From my point of view, I know I've already seen her many times from inside that borrowed body.

けれど彼女の心理を慮れば、『久し振り』という言葉が最も妥当だろうとオレは判断したのだ。%K%P
But, concerned about putting even more pressure on her psyche, I decided that "long time no see" was the most appropriate phrase to use.

黛「…………」%K%P
......

彼女はなにも、答えてはくれなかった。%K%P
She didn't respond.

目を細め、眺め透かすようにオレの顔を見ている。%K%P
With her eyes narrowed, she stared me as though trying to read my thoughts.

悟「ど、どうしたんだよ……」%K%P
W, what's wrong...?

悟「なんでそんな目で、見るんだ……？」%K%P
Why are you looking at me like that...?

あからさまな侮蔑の視線……。%K%P
A stare of open disdain....

冷たい表情に、オレの心まで凍える。%K%P
Her expression was so cold, I could feel it freezing me all the way to the heart.

なにか嫌な予感がした。%K%P
A feeling that something bad was about to happen overwhelmed me.

掻き乱されるように胸がざわめく。%K%P
My heart frantically pounded in my chest.

時が止まった。今はもう波の音さえ聞こえない。%K%P
Time stood still. I couldn't even hear the sound of the waves anymore.

張り詰めた静寂の中、彼女が微かにくちを開いた。%K%P
Within that tense silence, she opened her mouth ever so slightly.

そうして告げられた言葉は……。%K%P
And when she spoke, she said....

黛「あなた、誰？」%K%P
Who... are you?

それは死の宣告に近い衝撃をオレにもたらしたのだった。%K%P

You're...

黛「悟じゃない……」%K%P
...not Satoru...

You're not... the Yukidoh Satoru I knew...

あたりの景色が一瞬にして色を失った。%K%P
In an instant, the color drained out of my world.
