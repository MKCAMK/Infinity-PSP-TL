#PARAGRAPHS_TRANSLATED:
#EN#1
Means "peace" or "tranquility" in Hebrew.%N
The Biblical Solomon from whom this name is derived was the third king of ancient Israel.%N
Solomon excelled because of his faith and obedience to God, and possessed great wisdom and intelligence with which he led Israel into a Golden Age.%N

#EN#2
However, in King Solomon's later years, he placed heavy taxation upon his people in order to construct a golden temple for the Lord, a task that his father David was unable to accomplish.%N
As a result, soon after his death, Israel split into two northern and southern kingdoms.
