#EN#1
(a.k.a. French Foreign Legion)%N
A unit in the French Army. Created in 1831 by Louis Philippe, the then King of the French.%N
The purpose of its creation was to reduce wartime casualties amongst native French soldiers by using mercenaries from other countries and foreign nationals instead.%N
The Mercenary Legion troops know not of the word "surrender," nor are they taught how to act when captured; they are trained to fight to the death.%N

#EN#2
The Mercenary Legion has fought in many conflicts such as the French conquest of Algeria, the Indochina War and the Algerian Civil War; many foreign soldiers lost their lives in these conflicts.%N
Even now, any French troops dispatched overseas are almost always from the Mercenary Legion.%N
They were recently deployed in areas such as Afghanistan and Kosovo.
