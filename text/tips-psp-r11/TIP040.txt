#PARAGRAPHS_TRANSLATED:
#EN#1
In accordance with both the Criminal Procedure Code and the Japanese Constitution, the suspect or defendant in any case is entitled to legal counsel. However, though everyone has the right to counsel, in reality, there are many people who are too poor to hire an attorney to defend themselves.%N

#EN#2
Therefore, as written in the Constitution, "When a defendant is not able to appoint an attorney, that duty falls upon the state" (article 37, paragraph 3).%N
Thus, in cases where the defendant's poverty prevents them from being able to seek the services of an attorney, the court will appoint a public defender to assist them, with the cost being borne by the state.

#CN#1
以宪法的内容为基准，%N
刑事诉讼法中规定嫌疑犯或是被告人，%N
无论何时都可以雇用属于自己的辩护人。%N
然而，虽然有权雇用辩护人，%N
对于那些没有钱支付辩护费用的贫穷民众来说，%N
事实上相当于无法雇用律师替自己辩护。%N

#CN#2
因此，日本宪法规定%N
“当被告人无法自行雇用辩护人时，%N
由国家代为雇用”（三十七条三项）%N
在因为贫困而无法雇用辩护人的情况下，%N
由法院挑选、由国家经费雇用的辩护人%N
就称为法院指定的律师。%N
