
観相学
頭部の骨相や、目鼻立ちを見ることで、
その人の性格・運命を判断する占い。
</pre>
<pre>
A divination method, supposed to be able to foretell a person's character and destiny
based on examination of their cranial structure and facial features.%N
Viewed as a serious school of scientific thought during the Victorian era,
but has since been disproved and is now viewed as a quack medical practice.
